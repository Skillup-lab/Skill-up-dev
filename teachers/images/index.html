Welcome to Skill Up

Learn Anything, Teach Everything.
The most powerful peer-to-peer learning platform ever built.

What is Skill Up?

An EdTech platform that connects learners with local, verified tutors for in-person or virtual classes.

Think of Skill Up as a blend of:

Uber for tutors

Connecting nearby learners & teachers with ease and convenience.

Marketplace for local learning

Focused on practical, skill-based learning that matters in your community.

Community-driven platform

Centered on growth, mentorship, and support for both learners and teachers.

Our Mission

To make quality education accessible, practical skills teachable, and local talent visible — starting from the grassroots.

We believe:

Every skill matters.

Every learner deserves guidance.

Every teacher deserves the tools to teach.

How It Works

A simple workflow that connects learners with teachers in just a few steps.

For Students

1

Visit Skill Up website

Access our platform from any device, anytime.

2

Register on Skill up

Create Your Student Account on our Platform.

3

Find a Skill & Connect

Find a skill and connect with a verified teacher near you.

4

Book a session

Schedule your class and make payment (currently via WhatsApp).

5

Attend the class

Learn in-person or online, based on your preference.

6

Get Your Certificate

After completing your class and meeting all requirements,
you’ll receive a certificate of participation or completion.

7

Submit feedback

Help improve the platform quality with your honest review.

For Teachers

1

Visit Skill Up website

Access our platform from any device, anytime.
2

Register on Skill Up

Create your profile as a teacher on our platform.

3

Submit credentials

Share your expertise and the skill categories you can teach.

4

Get approved and listed

Once verified, your profile becomes visible to potential students.

5

Receive bookings

Teach students

Platform Features

Our Platform includes everything you need to start learning or teaching right away.

Explore the Platform

Learn how Skill Up works, browse featured skills, and get inspired to start your learning journey.

Sign Up Easily

Create a learner or teacher profile in minutes and become part of a growing learning network.

Find What You Need

Search by skill, category, or location to discover teachers and learning opportunities near you.

Request & Join Classes

Submit a class request and get matched to a teacher who meets your needs, schedule, and location.

Track Your Progress

Stay updated with your ongoing classes, upcoming lessons, and learning history — all in your dashboard.
