About Skill Up

We're on a mission to transform education by connecting local talent with eager learners, one skill at a time.

Our Story

How a simple idea to connect local teachers with students grew into a movement.

From Idea to Impact

Skill Up began in 2022 when our founder, Adebayo Ogunlesi, noticed a disconnect in his community: skilled professionals and artisans struggled to find students, while eager learners couldn't find quality teachers for practical skills.

What started as a simple WhatsApp group connecting math tutors with students in Lagos has evolved into a comprehensive platform addressing the educational needs of communities across Nigeria.

Our approach is intentionally grassroots and human-centered. We believe that technology should enhance human connection, not replace it. That's why Skill Up focuses on facilitating real, in-person learning experiences whenever possible.

Our Journey

The key milestones that have shaped Skill Up's evolution.

January 2022

Idea conception: WhatsApp group connecting math tutors with JAMB students in Lagos.

April 2022

Expanded to include language tutors and vocational skills like tailoring and cooking.

August 2022

First prototype of Skill Up website launched with 50 registered teachers.

January 2023

Reached 500 successful student-teacher matches across 5 cities in Nigeria.

June 2023

Secured seed funding to improve platform and expand operations.

Today

Growing community of teachers and learners with plans to expand across Africa.

Our Vision

A world where anyone can learn any skill from someone in their community, where teaching is valued as a profession, and where practical education is accessible to all.

We envision Skill Up as the bridge connecting Africa's vast talent pool with those eager to learn, creating economic opportunities and fostering community development.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Skill Up.

Community First

We believe in the power of local communities and prioritize connections that strengthen neighborhoods and towns.

Accessibility

Education should be available to everyone, regardless of background, location, or economic status.

Growth Mindset

We believe in continuous learning and improvement, both for our users and for ourselves as an organization.

Trust & Safety

We prioritize creating a safe environment where learners and teachers can connect with confidence.

Passion

We're passionate about education and believe that teaching and learning should be joyful experiences.

Fairness

We ensure fair compensation for teachers while keeping education affordable for learners.
